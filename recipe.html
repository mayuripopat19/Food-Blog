Avocado Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook fettuccine according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a blender, combine avocados, heavy cream, Parmesan, garlic, and lemon juice. Blend until smooth.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss the pasta with the avocado Alfredo sauce until evenly coated.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve immediately.

Beans Bourguignon

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked kidney beans
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, saut√© onions, carrots, and garlic until soft.
  2. Add tomato paste, thyme, and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour in red wine and let it reduce by half.
  4. Add vegetable broth, beans, and bay leaf, then simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, remove bay leaf, and serve hot.
